## Features

### Visual Indicators
- ✅ Success checkmarks
- ⚠️  Warning symbols
- ❌ Error symbols
- 📄 File operations
- 💰 Financial operations
- 🤖 AI/LLM operations
- ⚙️  Processing operations
- 📊 Statistics
- 💡 Tips and guidance

### Progress Bar Components
- **Filled:** █ (completed portion)
- **Empty:** ░ (remaining portion)
- **Percentage:** Shows completion percentage
- **Count:** Shows current/total items
- **Stage name:** Describes current operation

### Time Formatting
- Under 1 second: `500ms`
- Under 1 minute: `45s`
- Over 1 minute: `2m 15s`

### Error Limiting
- Shows up to 5 errors in summary
- Indicates if more errors exist: "... and 10 more"
- Full error details logged during processing
